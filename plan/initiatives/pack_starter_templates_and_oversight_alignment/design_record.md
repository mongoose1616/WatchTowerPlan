# Pack Starter Templates And Oversight Alignment Design Record

## Summary
Publishes reusable hosted-pack starter templates and aligns WatchTowerOversight to the shared core-host-pack contract.

## Initial Design Boundary
- The initiative package is machine-first and local to `plan/initiatives/pack_starter_templates_and_oversight_alignment/.wt/`.
- Authored intake docs remain editable inputs but require explicit machine confirmation.
- Readiness must fail closed on missing capture, blocking deferred items, open discrepancies, or missing approval.
